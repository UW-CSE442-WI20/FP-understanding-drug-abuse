We can clearly see that Marijuana is the top runner in drug popularity. A survey done in 2018 shows that more than 43 million citizens have used cannabis product in the past month. That’s around 15 percent of the US population. Several reasons contribute to this matter.
Marijuana legalization have led to more cannabis use and perhaps addiction — highlighting a public health downside to a policy change that now 11 states and Washington, DC, have adopted and several others are considering. Another reason Marijuana is becoming mainstream is that lots of people think its harmless.
